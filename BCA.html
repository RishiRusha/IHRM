Bachalor Of Computer Application(BCA)


Information Technology is the fastest growing revenue earner in India today. The Bachelor of Computer Application develops theoretical and practical abilities in a range of disciplines applicable for the gamut of work in IT. There is also sufficient emphasis on field projects for acquiring practical experience.


Course Features

Duration : Full-time, 3-year degree program
