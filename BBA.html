Bachelor Of Business Administration (BBA)


The Bachelor of Business Administration develops theoretical and practical ability in a range of business activities. The program also assists the student in understanding and developing the unique leadership qualities required for successfully managing business functions, an organizational unit or an enterprise.


Course Features

Eligibility : A student having passed 12th standard and having English as one of the subjects.

Duration : Full-time, 3-year degree program

Fees : 15,0000 INR
